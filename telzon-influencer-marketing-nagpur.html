Why use local Nagpur influencers instead of national ones?
Local Nagpur nano and micro influencers have audiences that are 80–90% Nagpur-based. Their followers trust their recommendations on local restaurants, gyms, stores and services far more than national influencers who have no geographic connection to your market.
How do you verify that influencers have real Nagpur audiences?
We check every influencer account with analytics tools to verify follower location, engagement rate authenticity and audience demographics before recommending them. We reject any accounts with fake followers or primarily non-Nagpur audiences.
What is the minimum budget for an influencer campaign?
A nano-influencer campaign with 3–5 local Nagpur influencers can start from ₹5,000–₹10,000 total including Telzon management fees. Micro-influencer campaigns typically start at ₹20,000 for a meaningful campaign.
Do you handle the content brief and coordination?
Yes. We handle everything end-to-end — influencer identification, outreach, contract, content brief writing, creative review approval, posting schedule coordination and final performance report. You just approve the brief before it goes live.
How do you measure influencer campaign results?
We track reach, impressions, engagement rate, story views, link clicks, swipe-ups and promo code redemptions. A detailed campaign report with all metrics is delivered within 48 hours of campaign completion.
