Instagram Reels + Google Local SEO + WhatsApp Loyalty: +85% Weekend Revenue in 90 Days

A Collingwood café with outstanding specialty coffee and a loyal but small customer base had built no digital infrastructure. Their Google Business Profile listing had minimal reviews, their Instagram was irregularly posted with inconsistent aesthetics, and their Uber Eats listing had stock photography. Despite being in one of Melbourne's most food-active suburbs, they were invisible to the suburb's constant stream of new visitors and weekend explorers.

TELZON deployed a three-pillar Melbourne growth system: first, a brand identity refresh that captured authentic Collingwood café culture — consistent visual language applied across the Instagram feed, Google Business Profile photography and delivery platform listings. Second, a Reels strategy targeting Melbourne's café discovery audience on Instagram Explore — four videos per month capturing the space, coffee process and team, optimised for location-tagged discovery. Third, a WhatsApp loyalty programme that collected customer contacts from in-venue QR codes and deployed automated weekly specials and birthday offers.

At 90 days: weekend revenue increased 85% against the baseline. Instagram following grew 340% with organic reach expanding beyond Collingwood to Melbourne-wide café audiences. Google Business Profile reviews grew from 31 to 120+, moving the listing into the top 3 for "Collingwood café". Menulog organic order volume increased without any additional promotional spend.
