## FREE TOOLS TO TRACK YOUR BACKLINKS
- **Google Search Console** → Links report (see who links to you)
- **Ahrefs Free Backlink Checker** — spy on competitors' links, copy them
- **Ubersuggest (free tier)** — find link opportunities
- **Google Alerts** — set alert for "Telzon Marketing" to catch unlinked mentions (then ask for a link)

---

### AVOID (these get you penalized)
- ❌ Buying bulk "10,000 backlinks" from Fiverr — Google penalty
- ❌ PBNs (private blog networks)
- ❌ Same exact anchor text everywhere — vary it
- ❌ Spammy comment links
- ❌ Irrelevant foreign directories with no traffic

Quality > quantity. 20 real, relevant links beat 1,000 spam links.
