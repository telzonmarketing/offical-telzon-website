Questions Answered
How can a café in Nagpur increase footfall during off-peak hours?
We create time-slot specific Instagram content and WhatsApp offers targeting 3–6pm — the dead hours for most cafés. Happy-hour promotions, combo deals and Reels pushed during those windows consistently drive afternoon footfall for our clients.
How do you build repeat customers for a café?
We build WhatsApp CRM broadcast lists of existing customers and run systematic loyalty campaigns — weekly specials, birthday offers and new menu alerts. Cafés using our WhatsApp CRM see 35–45% repeat customer rates within 90 days.
Can you help rank my café higher on food delivery platforms?
Yes. Delivery platform SEO involves optimising your menu listing titles, descriptions, cuisine tags, pricing display and cover photos. We also engineer your menu to highlight high-margin items and use promotional tools to boost ranking without ad spend.
How much does café marketing cost in Nagpur?
Café marketing packages start at ₹9,000/month covering Instagram management, WhatsApp CRM and delivery platform optimisation. Full packages with food photography, Reels production and influencer tie-ups start at ₹20,000/month.
Do you work with home-based bakeries in Nagpur?
Absolutely. Many of our clients are home bakers and cloud kitchens. We set up Instagram, WhatsApp ordering systems and Google Business profiles that let home bakeries compete with established cafés.
