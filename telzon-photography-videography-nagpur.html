FAQ
Photography & Video
Questions Answered
How long does a food photography shoot take?
A standard food photography session covers 15–25 dishes in 4–6 hours at your restaurant or our studio in Manish Nagar. We handle all food styling, lighting setup and props. No advance preparation needed from your side.
Do you follow food delivery platforms photo guidelines?
Yes. All food photos are delivered in food delivery platforms approved formats and resolutions. We know exactly what angles and styling convert to higher order volumes on these platforms and shoot accordingly.
What equipment do you use?
We shoot with full-frame Sony and Canon DSLR/mirrorless cameras, professional studio flash and LED lighting, and DJI stabilized video rigs. All video is shot in 4K for maximum quality and future-proofing.
How many edited photos will I receive?
A half-day food shoot delivers 30–40 fully retouched images. A full-day shoot delivers 60–80 images. All images are professionally colour graded and delivered in both web (2000px) and high-resolution (5000px+) versions.
Can you come to my business location in Nagpur?
Yes. We do on-location shoots across all Nagpur areas — Dharampeth, Sitabuldi, Manish Nagar, Wardha Road, Hingna and everywhere in between. Travel within Nagpur is included in the package price.
