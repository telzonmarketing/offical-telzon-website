FAQ
Branding Questions Answered
How much does logo design cost in Nagpur?
Logo design starts from ₹8,000 for a basic logo with 2 concepts. Full brand identity packages including logo, colour system, typography, business cards and brand guidelines start from ₹25,000. Custom pricing available for complex requirements.
What is included in a brand identity package?
A full brand identity package includes: primary logo + variations, colour palette with hex/CMYK/Pantone codes, typography system, business card design, letterhead design, social media templates (Instagram/Facebook) and a brand guidelines PDF document.
How long does branding take?
Logo-only: 7–10 business days. Full brand identity: 3–4 weeks including 3 rounds of revisions. Rush delivery (3–5 days) available at a premium. Timeline shared at project start.
Do you design menus and restaurant branding?
Yes. We specialise in F&B branding — restaurant logos, menu design, packaging, table tents, uniforms and outdoor signage. We have designed for 40+ restaurants and cafés across Nagpur.
Can you rebrand my existing Nagpur business?
Yes. We handle complete rebrands including auditing your existing brand, identifying what to keep and what to change, and rolling out the new identity across all touchpoints. A Dharampeth restaurant we rebranded saw 60% more walk-ins within 3 months.
