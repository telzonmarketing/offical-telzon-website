Questions Answered
When is the best time to start admission marketing in Nagpur?
The peak admission season in Nagpur is March–June. We recommend starting campaigns in January–February to build brand awareness before the decision-making period. Schools should start even earlier for the new academic year.
What is the cost per lead for coaching centres in Nagpur?
Our average CPL for coaching centres via Google Ads is ₹28–45. Facebook Lead Ads generate leads at ₹40–80. WhatsApp campaigns to targeted parent broadcast lists can generate leads at under ₹20 per enquiry.
Can Google Ads work for coaching centres and small institutes?
Absolutely. Parents and students search Google extensively for coaching in Nagpur. Keywords like "best JEE coaching Nagpur" or "NEET classes Dharampeth" have high search volume during March–June. We dominate these searches for our clients.
How do you reach parents on WhatsApp for admissions?
We build targeted parent broadcast lists through your existing database and lead generation campaigns. We then create multi-message WhatsApp sequences with fee structure PDFs, faculty highlights, student results and deadline reminders. WhatsApp has 95%+ open rates.
Do you create campus tour videos for colleges?
Yes. We produce professional YouTube campus tour videos and Instagram Reels showcasing facilities, faculty and student testimonials. Campus videos are highly effective for attracting out-of-city students and international families to your institution.
