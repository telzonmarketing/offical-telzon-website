Complete Restaurant Transformation: food delivery apps 3.2 → 4.5, +180% Orders

A 3-year-old multi-cuisine restaurant in Nagpur was struggling despite excellent food. Their food delivery rating was 3.2 (bad phone photos, no review responses), they had 0 Instagram followers and no WhatsApp marketing.

We started with a professional food photography shoot (32 dishes), rewrote all food delivery apps menu descriptions, fixed cuisine tags, set up systematic review response and launched a review solicitation WhatsApp follow-up. Then we launched Instagram with daily stories, 3 Reels per week and a WhatsApp broadcast list of 1,200 regular customers.

Within 90 days, their food delivery rating climbed from 3.2 to 4.5. Instagram grew from 0 to 15,000. Weekly food delivery apps + delivery platforms orders increased 180% and monthly revenue grew from ₹3L to ₹6.5L — an additional ₹3.5L per month.
