Is digital marketing legal for clinics and hospitals in India?
Yes, within ethical guidelines. We follow IMDA (Indian Medical Digital Association) guidelines and NMC regulations. We focus on educational content, appointment generation and reputation management — never prohibited content like guaranteed cure claims.
How do you improve a clinic's local search ranking in Nagpur?
local search ranking for clinics depends on profile completeness, photo quality, review volume, response rate and local citation consistency. We systematically optimise all these factors over 60–90 days, with measurable improvements by Month 2.
Can you manage negative patient reviews professionally?
Yes. We monitor all reviews on Google, Practo and Justdial. We respond professionally to negative reviews within 24 hours using IMDA-compliant language, and implement a systematic process to encourage satisfied patients to share their experience.
Do you run Google Ads for clinics in Nagpur?
Yes. Google Ads for clinics target symptom-based and specialty searches. We run Call-Only ads so patients can call directly and Search ads linking to appointment booking pages. We follow Google healthcare advertising policies and NMC guidelines strictly.
How long until we see more patients from digital marketing?
Google Business Profile optimisation typically shows ranking improvements within 60–90 days. Google Ads begin delivering appointment calls within 2 weeks. SEO content takes 3–6 months for significant organic traffic. Most healthcare clients see a measurable patient increase within 45–60 days.
